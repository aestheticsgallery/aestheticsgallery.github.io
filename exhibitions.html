Solo Exhibition Music 'n' Moods

The first solo exhibition by artist Jyoti Gupta showcasing the collection in acrylic and oil colours inspired by music and sounds. The exhibition provides unique opportunity for a sensual journey through art and music.

Music rejuvenates mind and soul. Every art piece, gives one a moment to pause and rely on senses and feelings of artist.

The work transforms the visual rhythm of strokes and colours into melodies. Paintings are uncomplicated, balanced and satisfying with a simple expression of colours.
