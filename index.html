About Jyoti

Since my childhood I have had passion for art, but I never learned it technically. Thus, my art is captured when there is a spontaneous movement of my brush and it swirls and meets the canvas. Through my art, I try to capture the theme and transform it into senses. My goal is to inspire those who see my art through the simple expression of colours. Taking that forward, I attempt to convey my feelings and emotions through visual reality.
